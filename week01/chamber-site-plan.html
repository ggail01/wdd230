Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name:

Moises Padilla Chamber of Commerce

Reason: This name was selected because it is my hometown, and also to represent the vibrant and growing business community of Moises Padilla, Negros Occidental.

Site Purpose:

The Moises Padilla Chamber of Commerce website aims to promote local businesses, facilitate community development, and serve as a hub for business networking and resources.

Scenarios:

  • How can local businesses benefit from joining the Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the upcoming networking events in Moises Padilla?
  • Where can I find information about local business regulations and resources?

Color Schema:

#0A3044 #87CEEB #708090 #F8F8F8

Primary Color (#0A3044): Used for backgrounds and dominant elements.

Secondary Color (#87CEEB): Used for supporting elements.

Accent Colors: #708090 (Slate gray) and #F8F8F8 (Off-white) are for highlights.
